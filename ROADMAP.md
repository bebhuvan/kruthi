# Roadmap (v2)

## North Star
Kruthi should make classic reading calm, local-first, and quote-grounded across devices.

## Principles
- Local-first by default.
- Quote-first answers with clear evidence.
- Keep reading flow uninterrupted.
- Cross-platform parity where it matters.
- Privacy stays simple and explicit.

## v2 Goals (must-haves)
1. Reading experience polish
   - Faster open, search, and page rendering.
   - Better typography controls and layout presets.
   - Improved TOC, footnotes, and in-book navigation.
2. Library and highlights power
   - Tags, filters, and fast bulk actions.
   - Richer notes on highlights.
   - Better export (Markdown, print, share).
3. Companion quality
   - Clear citation viewer and evidence map.
   - Provider selection and model presets.
   - Better caching to avoid repeat costs.
4. Reliability and performance
   - Smaller bundle sizes and faster boot.
   - Offline downloads and resilient storage.
   - Crash reporting with opt-in.
5. Distribution and docs
   - Predictable release cadence.
   - Auto-update for desktop.
   - Website and onboarding guide.

## Milestones
### M1 - Reading Flow
- Typography and layout presets.
- Search speed and TOC improvements.
- Reader performance work.

### M2 - Highlights and Knowledge
- Tagging, filtering, and bulk edits.
- Export upgrades and print views.
- Highlight note enhancements.

### M3 - Companion and Context
- Citation viewer and evidence map.
- Multi-chapter context options.
- Provider presets and cost controls.

### M4 - Reliability and Distribution
- Desktop auto-update.
- Release checklist and CI polish.
- Website launch.

## Stretch Goals
- Optional encrypted sync across devices.
- Reading goals and gentle streaks.
- Audio reading and pronunciation helpers.

## Not Planned (v2)
- DRM-protected ebook support.
- Full social reading network features.
